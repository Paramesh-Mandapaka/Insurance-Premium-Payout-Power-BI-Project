# Insurance Premium & Payout Power BI Project

An **interactive Power BI dashboard** designed for an **Insurance Company** to analyze **premium collections, policyholder details, claims, payouts, and investments** ‚Äî all in one place. 

This project showcases the power of **data analytics, visualization, and business intelligence** in driving strategic decisions.

![Dashboard](Insurence%20Overview.PNG)

---

## Project Overview

This **Power BI report** provides a complete analytical view of an insurance company‚Äôs performance.  

It helps business leaders, financial teams, and analysts make **data-driven decisions** by tracking KPIs such as **premium trends, customer demographics, claim ratios, and investment outcomes.**

 It visualizes crucial KPIs such as:
 
- **Total Premium Collected**
- **Policy Count by Category**
- **Payout & Claim Ratios**
- **Investment vs Maturity Returns**
- **Annual Premium vs Protection Value**
- **Sales Performance by Hierarchy**
- **Policyholder Demographics & Details**

This dashboard enables management and analysts to identify business trends, assess financial performance, and optimize strategies for better growth and customer retention.
