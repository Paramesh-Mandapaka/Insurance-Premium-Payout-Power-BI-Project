# Insurance Premium & Payout Power BI Project
This **Power BI Project** provides deep insights into the performance and key metrics of an **Insurance Company**, focusing on premium collections, payouts, policyholder distribution, and investment analysis.

![Dashboard](Insurence%20Overview.PNG)

---

## Project Overview

The **Insurance Premium & Payout Dashboard** is an interactive and data-driven report designed to support decision-making across different departments of an insurance company. It visualizes crucial KPIs such as:

- **Total Premium Collected**
- **Policy Count by Category**
- **Payout & Claim Ratios**
- **Investment vs Maturity Returns**
- **Annual Premium vs Protection Value**
- **Sales Performance by Hierarchy**
- **Policyholder Demographics & Details**

This dashboard enables management and analysts to identify business trends, assess financial performance, and optimize strategies for better growth and customer retention.
